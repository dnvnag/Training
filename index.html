Introduction

Industry Requirement

For next few years to come, industry will go through a major transformation in "Digitization" of the way they conduct their business, by adopting cloud, blockchain, automation, Artificial Inetelligence (AI) and Robotics. In order to meet this requirement, those who are going to be associated with IT industry as well as those who are alaready Professionals need to go through a "New Learning Curve" to keep themselves relevant in the industry. This course is an enabler for those who would like to go through this "Digitization Journey". This is the first of the series of course, starting with cloud as most of the solutions are moving to cloud
